The story

I don’t have a deep backstory for these — I just know they clicked immediately. The colourway is understated but interesting, and they somehow work with almost everything without ever feeling boring.

These ended up becoming my favourite Jordan 1s in my collection, which surprised me a bit considering how quiet they are compared to some others.

I swapped the laces to orange to match the swoosh and accent colours, and that small change made them feel completely mine.

Quick notes

Standout detail: The muted green paired with the orange accents

Modification: Orange lace swap to match the swoosh

Why I keep them: They’re easy, comfortable, and never get old
