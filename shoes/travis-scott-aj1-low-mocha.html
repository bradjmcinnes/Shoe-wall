The story

After I got the Reverse Mochas, it was basically over. I knew I wanted the original Mochas too. The Reverse pair is easy and clean, but the original has that “this is the one” energy.

It’s the same Travis formula — reverse swoosh, earth tones, premium materials — but it feels moodier and more iconic. It doesn’t need loud colours to stand out. It just looks expensive.

Why it was inevitable

This is one of those shoes where you can understand the hype without needing to be told. Once you have one Travis low, you start seeing why people chase them — and then you start justifying things you absolutely don’t need.

I’m not even going to pretend this was a “smart” purchase. It was a “complete the set” purchase. And I stand by it.

Quick notes

Standout detail: The original mocha colour blocking + reverse swoosh

Vibe: Slightly darker, slightly more iconic than the Reverse Mocha

Personal meaning: Reverse Mocha started it — this one made it official
