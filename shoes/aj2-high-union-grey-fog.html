Union x Air Jordan 2 High “Grey Fog”

DN3802-001
First Union Design-forward Natural progression

The context

Once I developed an appreciation for the Jordan 2, my first Union collaboration choice was basically a no-brainer. The Grey Fogs felt like the cleanest intersection of a silhouette I already loved and a brand known for doing things thoughtfully.

This wasn’t about chasing a Union collab just to say I owned one, it made sense naturally within my rotation.

The Union touch

The Grey Fog colourway leans into subtlety. Muted tones, layered materials, and small details that reward actually looking at the shoe rather than just recognizing it from across the room.

It’s unmistakably a Union shoe, but it doesn’t overwhelm the Jordan 2’s already refined shape. Instead, it amplifies what makes the model interesting in the first place.

Why it works for me

These slid into my rotation easily. They feel intentional without feeling precious, and they fit right alongside the rest of my Jordan 2s without trying to steal the spotlight.

If the Lucky Greens are worn deliberately, the Grey Fogs are worn confidently.

Quick notes

Standout detail: Union’s layered materials and subtle branding

Vibe: Calm, considered, and very wearable

Personal milestone: First Union collab — and the right one
