Air Jordan 4 “Black Cat” (2025)

Air Jordan 4
Monochrome Intentional hold Future rotation

The pair

These are the 2025 Black Cat 4s — not the 2020 release — and they’re exactly what the name promises: all black, no distractions, no unnecessary contrast.

The appeal here is restraint. The silhouette does all the work, and the colourway lets it speak without anything competing for attention.

The lace swap

I swapped in Champs Blue laces, which adds just enough contrast to break up the black without ruining the point of the shoe. It’s subtle, but once you see it, it changes the whole feel.

Why I’m not wearing them (yet)

As much as I like them, I have no intention of wearing these for a while. Right now, there are just too many people wearing Black Cats.

This is one of those pairs I’m deliberately letting cool off. In a few years, when the hype cycle moves on and they feel less omnipresent, they’ll hit a lot harder.

Quick notes

Standout detail: All-black execution that lets the AJ4 shape shine

Lace choice: Champs Blue — subtle contrast

Rotation plan: Not now. Later.
