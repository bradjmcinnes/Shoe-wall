Air Jordan 1 Low “Nu Retro Palomino”

DZ5485-020
Underrated Resale disaster Personal favourite

The story

This is one of those shoes that absolutely nobody seemed to want. So much so that I found them on StockX for $68; cheap enough that the reseller was clearly willing to lose money just to get rid of them.

I loved them immediately. The colour combo, the materials, the overall vibe — it all worked for me, even if it clearly didn’t work for the market.

The “Neapolitan” shoe

Once I swapped in pink laces, it was over. Between the white, brown, and pink tones, people started calling these the “Neapolitan” shoes, and honestly, they’re not wrong.

It’s one of those accidental nicknames that fits so well it just sticks.

Why I love them

These are a reminder that resale value has nothing to do with whether a shoe is actually good. Sometimes a shoe just misses with the crowd, and sometimes that makes it even better.

I didn’t buy these because they were popular. I bought them because I liked them. And that’s exactly why they earn a spot on the wall.

Quick notes
