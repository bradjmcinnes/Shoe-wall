Air Jordan 4 “Cave Stone”

FV5029-200
First AJ4 Neutral tones Easy entry

The milestone

These were my first Jordan 4s. Not a loud colourway, not a hype-driven pick — just a clean, neutral entry into a silhouette I hadn’t owned before.

Looking back, it was actually the perfect way to start with AJ4s.

Why Cave Stone worked

The Cave Stone colourway is understated in the best way. Earthy, muted tones that let the shape of the shoe do the talking without pulling attention in ten directions.

As a first AJ4, it made the silhouette feel approachable instead of intimidating. Easy to style, easy to wear, and easy to understand.

In hindsight

Starting with a neutral pair made it a lot easier to appreciate what the Jordan 4 actually is — the structure, the panels, the way it sits on foot.

Everything that came after builds on this one.

Quick notes

Standout detail: Earth-tone palette that works with almost anything

Role in the collection: First step into AJ4s

Why it matters: The beginning of the Jordan 4 chapter
