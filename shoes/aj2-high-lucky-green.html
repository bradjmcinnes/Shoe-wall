Air Jordan 2 High “Lucky Green”

Air Jordan 2 High
Favourite pair First StockX purchase Worn intentionally

The feeling

These are, without question, my favourite shoes that I own.

I don’t even have a clean, logical explanation for it, I just fell in love with them immediately. The colour, the shape, the balance of the green against the white… something about it just clicked.

First StockX purchase

This was also my first-ever StockX purchase, which probably adds to the attachment. There’s something about taking that first step, trusting the process, waiting for the box, opening it, that makes a pair stick with you.

Why I don’t wear them often

I don’t throw these on casually. In fact, I can almost mark on the calendar every time I’ve worn them. Not because I’m afraid of damaging them, but because I like keeping them special.

Every time I wear them, it feels deliberate. Like, “today is a Lucky Green day.”

Quick notes

Standout detail: The green hits — bold without being loud

Personal meaning: Favourite pair, no contest

Wear philosophy: Not often — but always intentionally
